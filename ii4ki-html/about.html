what I do

I build small, focused tools that solve problems I keep running into. Most of them are personal automation: bash scripts, AI-assisted pipelines, a statusline here, a secrets layer there. None of them are products. All of them save me time every day.

My current obsession is figuring out which parts of my workflow can be handed to an agent without losing the things I actually enjoy about software.

what I write about

bash, secrets, Claude Code, agent workflows, ops. Practical posts about the boring scaffolding that makes the interesting work possible.
